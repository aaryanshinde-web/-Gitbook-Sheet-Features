# Dashboard Quick Pay

## Summary

Dashboard Quick Pay enables members to initiate a transfer or payment directly from the Dashboard landing screen — without entering the Move Money workflow. For business members who make the same recurring transfers daily or need to fund an account immediately after a balance check, Quick Pay reduces the payment initiation path to a single widget interaction, compressing a multi-step process into seconds.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Quick Pay to fund a checking account from savings before an end-of-day ACH batch runs, transfer operating funds between accounts, or make a loan payment when the upcoming payment widget signals an approaching due date. Members managing multiple accounts use Quick Pay as the fastest path to move money without navigating to Move Money > Transfer. The widget also serves members making time-sensitive payments to other members where waiting to navigate through the full transfer flow would introduce unnecessary delay.

## End-to-End Workflow

### Step 1 — Locate the Quick Transfer Widget

After logging in, the Quick Transfer widget is visible on the top-right of the Dashboard. It contains a dollar amount field, a "From account" dropdown, a "Send money to" dropdown, a Memo field, and a **Transfer** button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/dashboard-landing.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 2 — Enter the Transfer Amount

Click the dollar amount field in the Quick Transfer widget and type the amount to transfer (e.g., $100).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/quick-transfer-amount.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 3 — Select the "FROM" Account

Click the **"Send money From"** dropdown. A list of all accounts under the membership appears — Checking, Savings, Visa Credit Card, Mortgage, and Loan accounts — each showing the account name and number. Select the destination account.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/quick-transfer-select-to.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 4 — Select the "TO" account

Click the **"Send money TO"** dropdown. A list of all accounts under the membership appears — Checking, Savings, Visa Credit Card, Mortgage, and Loan accounts — each showing the account name and number. Select the destination account.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/quick-transfer-to-selected.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 5 — Click Transfer and Confirm

Click the **Transfer** button. A confirmation modal appears showing the transfer details: From account, To account, Amount, Frequency, and Transaction date. Review the details and click **Confirm** to complete the transfer, or click **Cancel** to go back.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/quick-transfer-confirm.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
