# Loan Payments & Quick Pay

> **Module:** Banking › Move Money → Quick Pay

## Summary

Quick Pay is an integrated loan payment hub embedded within the nFinia digital banking platform. It gives Summerville Credit Union members a single screen to view all active loan accounts and make payments without leaving the digital banking experience. The Single Sign-On (SSO) design means that members who are already authenticated in nFinia are automatically signed into the Quick Pay portal — no separate login required.

Members can pay the minimum amount due, a custom amount above the minimum, or a full payoff amount. Payments can be funded from any linked CU account. Members whose primary income deposits at a different institution can also link an external bank account specifically for loan repayment purposes, enabling direct debit from a non-CU checking account without requiring a separate ACH setup.

**At a Glance**

| Attribute | Detail |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Module | Move Money > Quick Pay (SSO) |
| Loan Types | Auto, Personal, Consumer, Mortgage, Line of Credit, Credit Card |
| Payment Sources | Any linked CU account; external account for loan repayments |
| Payment Options | Minimum due, custom amount, full payoff |
| Same-Day Posting | Available when submitted before the daily cut-off time |

## Key Use Cases

| Use Case | Who Uses It | What They Do | Business Value |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Minimum Monthly Payment** | Members making their required monthly payment | Select loan, enter minimum amount due, and confirm | Prevents late fees without requiring the member to recall the exact amount |
| **Extra Principal Payment** | Members paying above the minimum to reduce balance | Select loan, enter a custom amount above minimum, and confirm | Reduces outstanding loan balance and total interest paid over time |
| **Full Payoff** | Members paying off a loan entirely | Request a payoff quote, enter the exact payoff amount, and confirm | Clears the loan in a single transaction and closes the account |
| **External Account Loan Payment** | Members whose income deposits at another institution | Link an external bank account as the loan payment source | Enables loan payments from a non-CU account without a separate ACH arrangement |

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Navigate to Quick Pay**

Log in to Summerville Credit Union digital banking. From the top navigation bar, click **Move Money** to open the Move Money Hub, then select **Quick Pay**. Alternatively, use the search bar and type "Quick Pay" to navigate directly to the feature.

The Quick Pay page loads and displays all active loan accounts associated with the member's profile. Each loan is listed with its account name, current balance, and minimum payment due. Select the loan account you want to make a payment towards to proceed.

**Step 2 — Make a Loan Payment**

After selecting a loan, the payment entry screen appears. This screen provides multiple payment options to accommodate different member needs:

- **Minimum Payment** — Pre-populated with the current minimum amount due. Select this option to pay only the required amount for the billing cycle.
- **Custom Amount** — Enter a specific dollar amount above the minimum to make an additional principal payment or pay more than the minimum due.
- **Full Payoff** — Select this option to request the current payoff balance and pay the loan in full.

Select the **payment source account** from the dropdown of linked CU accounts. If you have an external account linked for loan repayments, it will also appear as a payment source option. Once the payment details are confirmed, click **Submit Payment** to process the transaction. Payments submitted before the daily cut-off time are posted the same day.

**Step 3 — My Shares Tab**

The **My Shares** tab within Quick Pay displays all share accounts (savings and checking) available for use as payment sources. This view allows members to review account balances before selecting a source account, ensuring sufficient funds are available before submitting the loan payment. Members can switch between the Loans tab and the My Shares tab at any time to cross-reference balances and available funds.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (1) (1) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Members can also review a complete history of Quick Pay transactions by navigating to the **Transaction History** tab, view all active and completed **Scheduled Transfers**, and access **Notifications** under the More tab to see a log of all payment confirmations and alerts sent to their registered contact details.
