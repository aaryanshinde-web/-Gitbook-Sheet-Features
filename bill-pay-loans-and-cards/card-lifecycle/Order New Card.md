# Order a New Card

_Module: Banking › Cards › Open New Card_

## Summary

The Order a New Card feature lets you request a new debit or credit card — for example, to open a second card on an existing account, replace a worn card, or add a card with a different design. You can optionally order a physical copy in addition to the digital card, set an initial PIN, and the new card will be mailed to your address on file.

## At a Glance

| Attribute | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Module | Banking › Cards › Open New Card |
| Who Can Use | All nFinia Digital Banking members with an eligible account |
| Options | Debit / Credit, card design, physical copy yes/no, initial PIN |
| Delivery | Physical card mailed within ~5 business days |
| Digital Access | Digital card available immediately |
| Availability | 24 / 7 — via web or mobile |

## Key Use Cases

| Use Case | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Add a second card** | Order an additional card on an existing account |
| **Replace a worn card** | Order a fresh card without deactivating the current one |
| **Choose a new design** | Select a different card face at issuance |
| **Digital-only card** | Skip the physical copy if you only need digital wallet use |

## Step-by-Step Guide

_Navigation: Banking › Cards › Open New Card_

### Step 1 — Open the Cards Dashboard

From the top navigation, click **Cards** to open the Cards dashboard. In the top-right of the dashboard, click **⊕ Open New Card** to start the order workflow.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Cards_List.png" alt="Cards dashboard with Open New Card action" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 1: From the Cards dashboard, click <strong>Open New Card</strong> in the top-right corner.</p></figcaption></figure>

### Step 2 — Select Account, Card Type, and Options

The Order a New Card form appears with four sections: **Select Account Number**, **Select Card Type** (choose from available card designs), **Should you like a physical copy of the new card?** (Yes / No), and **Set pin for a new card** (enter and confirm an initial PIN). Fill in each section as required.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Order_New_Card_Form.png" alt="Order a New Card — configuration form" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 2: Select the account, card design, physical copy preference, and initial PIN.</p></figcaption></figure>

### Step 3 — Review and Submit

Once all required fields are complete, the **Order a New Card** button becomes active. Review your selections — particularly the card design and mailing preference — then click **Order a New Card** to submit the request.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Order_New_Card_Filled.png" alt="Order a New Card — ready to submit" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 3: Review the filled form and click <strong>Order a New Card</strong>.</p></figcaption></figure>

### Step 4 — Confirm the Order

A confirmation dialog appears asking **"Are you sure you want to order a new card?"** with a summary of the order. Click **Yes, Order a New Card** to place the order or **No, Cancel** to go back and adjust your selections.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Order_New_Card_Confirm.png" alt="Order a New Card — confirmation dialog" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 4: Confirm the order by clicking <strong>Yes, Order a New Card</strong>.</p></figcaption></figure>

### Step 5 — Order Placed

A success screen appears: **"Your new card has been ordered."** The screen shows the masked card number, the funding account, and the digital card image. Click **Go to Accounts** to return to your account list or **View Card Details** to jump directly to the new card's detail page.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Order_New_Card_Success.png" alt="Order confirmation — new card has been ordered" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 5: Order placed. The digital card is immediately available; the physical copy (if requested) arrives within ~5 business days.</p></figcaption></figure>

> **Note:** If you requested a physical copy, you will need to activate the physical card once it arrives — see the **Activate New Card** guide. The digital card is usable immediately via digital wallets.
