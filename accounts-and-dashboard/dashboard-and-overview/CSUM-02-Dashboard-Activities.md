# Dashboard Activities (CSUM-02)

## Summary

The Dashboard Activities view is the primary account monitoring screen in nFinia — combining an account balance summary, a recent transaction feed, and quick-action shortcuts into a single post-login destination. For business members who log in multiple times per day to track cash position, verify payment postings, and monitor for unexpected activity, this screen provides everything needed for a daily account health check without navigating into individual account detail screens.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Dashboard Activities as their daily opening screen — confirming that overnight ACH credits have posted, that scheduled payments have processed, and that account balances are within expected ranges before the business day begins. Members monitoring for fraud use the transaction feed to spot unfamiliar charges immediately on login, triggering an account inquiry before additional unauthorised transactions can occur. Operations staff reviewing a business member's account use the Activities view to see a consolidated snapshot of recent account movement across all linked accounts in a single screen, supporting faster issue identification and resolution.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Review the Account Balance Summary**

After authentication, the Dashboard displays a balance summary panel listing each linked account with its current and available balance. This panel is the fastest way to confirm funding levels across multiple accounts — business members checking whether a payroll account is adequately funded before a payroll ACH run will see the answer immediately without further navigation.

**Step 2 — Review Recent Transaction Activity**

Scroll to the Activities section to see recent transactions posted across all accounts in reverse chronological order. Each entry shows the account, transaction description, date, and amount — providing a cross-account activity feed that surfaces recent debits and credits in a single view.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/img_dd37cd06feee.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3 — Use Quick Action Shortcuts**

The Dashboard displays quick-action buttons — Transfer, Pay, Deposit — that launch the corresponding Move Money features directly. Business members initiating a transfer or payment without navigating through the full menu use these shortcuts to compress the workflow to two or three interactions from the Dashboard.
