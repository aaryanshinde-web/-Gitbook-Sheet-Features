# Transfer to Other Members

## Summary

Transfer to Other Members enables members to send funds directly to another Caltech Employees FCU member's account — settling instantly within the credit union's core system without requiring the recipient's external account details. For business members paying contractors, sole proprietors, or suppliers who also bank at Caltech Employees FCU, this is the fastest and lowest-friction internal transfer path — no routing numbers, no ACH delays, and no wire fees.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Member-to-Member transfers to pay a contractor or freelancer who banks at Caltech Employees FCU, funding their account instantly on the day the work is completed without the standard ACH processing delay. Members use this feature to split a shared expense with a family member or business partner at the same institution — looking up the recipient by name or member number rather than requiring account and routing details. The transfer also supports intra-family account funding scenarios common among Caltech Employees FCU members, where a parent regularly funds a college student's checking account within the same institution.
