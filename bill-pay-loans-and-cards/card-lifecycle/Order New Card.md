# Order New Card

## Summary

Order New Card allows members to request a new debit or credit card directly through nFinia Digital Banking — without visiting a branch or calling the credit union. For business members adding a new cardholder to a business account or replacing a card that was not the result of fraud, this self-service ordering flow initiates card production and delivery to the address on file in one session.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Order New Card to request an additional business debit card when onboarding a new authorised user to the company account, with the card issued and mailed without requiring a branch visit. Members whose card has expired use the ordering flow to request a new card if the automatic renewal was not received, confirming the mailing address before submitting the request. Operations staff managing a business account with multiple cardholders use Order New Card to track which cards are in production and which have been delivered, keeping the card inventory current without manual record-keeping.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Navigate to Order New Card**

From the Cards dashboard, click **Order New Card** in the card management menu or locate it under the card action options. The order form loads with the card type and account already pre-selected based on the account context.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Cards_List.png" alt="Cards dashboard with Open New Card action" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 1: From the Cards dashboard, click <strong>Open New Card</strong> in the top-right corner.</p></figcaption></figure>

**Step 2 — Confirm Delivery Address and Card Details**

Review the mailing address pre-populated from the account profile and update it if the card should be delivered to a different location. Confirm the card type and any available design options, then click **Submit Order** to initiate card production.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Order_New_Card_Form.png" alt="Order a New Card — configuration form" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 2: Select the account, card design, physical copy preference, and initial PIN.</p></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3 — Receive Order Confirmation**

A confirmation message displays the order reference number and the estimated delivery timeframe — typically 5–7 business days for standard delivery. The card will arrive in an inactive state and must be activated through digital banking or the phone number on the card sticker before first use.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Order_New_Card_Filled.png" alt="Order a New Card — ready to submit" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 3: Review the filled form and click <strong>Order a New Card</strong>.</p></figcaption></figure>
