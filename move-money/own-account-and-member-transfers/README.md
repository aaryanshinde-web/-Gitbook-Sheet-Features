# Own Account & Member Transfers

Own Account & Member Transfers covers funds movement that stays inside Summerville Credit Union — transfers between a member’s own accounts and transfers to other Summerville members. These are the fastest, lowest-friction transfer rails in nFinia and are settled in near real time.

## What's in this section

* **Transfer to Own Account & Scheduled** — — one-time and scheduled transfers between the member’s own Summerville accounts, including recurring series.
* **Transfer to Other Members** — — member-to-member transfers within Summerville CU, including recipient lookup and confirmation.
