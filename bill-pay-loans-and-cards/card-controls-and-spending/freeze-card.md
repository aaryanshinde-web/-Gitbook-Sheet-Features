# Freeze Card

## Summary

The Freeze Card feature allows members to instantly lock a debit or credit card — blocking all new transactions — without cancelling the card or requiring a replacement. For business members who misplace a company card or suspect unauthorised use, a card freeze is the fastest first response: it stops further charges immediately while the member investigates, and it can be reversed just as quickly if the card is found to be secure.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use the freeze feature when a company card is temporarily misplaced — freezing it immediately to prevent charges while the card is located, then unfreezing it once recovered without requesting a replacement or updating any payment credentials. Members who notice an unrecognised charge on a statement freeze the card as a precautionary measure while submitting a transaction inquiry, preventing additional fraudulent charges during the investigation period. Operations staff managing multiple business cards use the freeze as a standard off-boarding control — freezing a departing employee's card instantly upon termination without waiting for a physical card return.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Open Card Controls and Locate the Freeze Toggle**

From the Cards dashboard, click the card tile you want to freeze to open Card Controls. The **Freeze Card** toggle is displayed prominently at the top of the panel — the current card status (active or frozen) is shown alongside the toggle.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Cards_List.png" alt="Cards dashboard listing all user cards" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 1: Open the Cards dashboard and locate the card you want to freeze. Each row shows a Card Status toggle.</p></figcaption></figure>

**Step 2 — Toggle the Card Freeze On**

Click the **Freeze Card** toggle to lock the card immediately. All new purchase authorisations, ATM withdrawals, and contactless payments will be declined in real time until the freeze is lifted — existing recurring charges and scheduled payments are also blocked during the freeze period.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Freeze_Card_Confirm.png" alt="Freeze Card confirmation dialog" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 2: Review the confirmation message and click <strong>Yes, Freeze Card</strong> to freeze the card.</p></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3 — Unfreeze When Ready**

To restore card functionality, return to Card Controls and toggle **Freeze Card** off. The card becomes active immediately — no new card number is issued and no payment credentials change, so existing autopay setups and digital wallet tokens remain valid.
