# Move Money Hub

## Summary

The Move Money Hub is the central navigation screen for all payment and transfer capabilities in nFinia — internal transfers, external ACH, wire transfers, FedNow instant pay, bill pay, peer-to-peer payments, and loan payments are all accessible from this single entry point. For business members who use multiple payment rails depending on the transaction type and urgency, the Move Money Hub eliminates the need to remember where each payment method lives, presenting all options in a single organised layout.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use the Move Money Hub as the daily starting point for all fund movement decisions — selecting the appropriate payment rail based on the amount, timing requirement, and counterparty. Members managing multiple payment types in the same session navigate directly from the Hub to ACH for vendor payments, then to Wire for a same-day settlement, without retracing through the main navigation. Operations staff onboarding new members to digital banking use the Hub as the primary orientation point for demonstrating the full range of payment capabilities available through the platform.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Open the Move Money Hub**

From the top navigation, click **Move Money** to open the Move Money Hub. The screen displays all available payment and transfer options as clearly labelled tiles — Own Account Transfer, Transfer to Member, External Transfer, Wire Transfer, Instant Pay, Bill Pay, and Loan Payment.

**Step 2 — Select the Appropriate Payment Method**

Click the tile that matches the payment type required for the transaction. Each tile navigates directly to that payment module's entry form, pre-populated with the member's linked accounts where applicable.
