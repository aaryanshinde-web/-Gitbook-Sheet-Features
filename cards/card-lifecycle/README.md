# Card Lifecycle

Card Lifecycle covers card-level actions that span issuance through retirement — activating a new card, viewing card details, adding a card to a digital wallet (Apple Pay / Google Pay / Samsung Pay), deactivating or replacing a card, and more. These flows are available to Summerville CU members across both web and mobile.

## What's in this section

* **Visa Push Provisioning — Link Card to Digital Wallet** — The web-initiated push-provisioning flow that adds a Summerville CU card to the member's mobile wallet.
* **Add to Digital Wallet (Mobile)** — The in-app Add to Wallet flow on iOS and Android, including the Visa / Mastercard handshake steps.
* **Deactivate / Replace Card** — Permanently deactivate a card and optionally request a replacement with a new card number.
* **Activate New Card** — Activate a newly issued or replacement card using the card number, expiration date, and CVV.
