# Card Lifecycle

This subsection guides members through the complete lifecycle of their debit and credit cards — from initial activation through daily management and end-of-life replacement.

## Pages in this section

| Page | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| **[Activate New Card](<Activate New Card.md>)** | Activate a newly issued or replacement card from the Cards dashboard |
| **[Freeze Card](<Freeze Card.md>)** | Temporarily pause a card without permanent deactivation |
| **[Set Card PIN](<Set PIN.md>)** | Create or change the 4-digit PIN used at ATMs and chip-and-PIN terminals |
| **[Travel Notice](<Travel Notice.md>)** | Notify the credit union of upcoming travel and manage itineraries |
| **[Order a New Card](<Order New Card.md>)** | Order an additional or replacement card with optional physical copy |
| **[Link Card to Digital Wallet](<Link Card to Digital Wallet.md>)** | Add the card to Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Samsung Pay |
| **[Deactivate / Replace Card](<Deactivate Replace Card.md>)** | Permanently deactivate a card and optionally request a replacement |
