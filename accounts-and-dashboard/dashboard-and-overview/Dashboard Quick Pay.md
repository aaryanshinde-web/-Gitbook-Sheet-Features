# Dashboard Quick Pay

## Summary

Dashboard Quick Pay enables members to initiate a transfer or payment directly from the Dashboard landing screen — without entering the Move Money workflow. For business members who make the same recurring transfers daily or need to fund an account immediately after a balance check, Quick Pay reduces the payment initiation path to a single widget interaction, compressing a multi-step process into seconds.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Quick Pay to fund a checking account from savings before an end-of-day ACH batch runs, transfer operating funds between accounts, or make a loan payment when the upcoming payment widget signals an approaching due date. Members managing multiple accounts use Quick Pay as the fastest path to move money without navigating to Move Money > Transfer. The widget also serves members making time-sensitive payments to other members where waiting to navigate through the full transfer flow would introduce unnecessary delay.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Locate the Quick Pay Widget on the Dashboard**

After logging in, the Quick Pay widget is visible on the Dashboard with From and To account selectors and an Amount field. Select the source account, the destination account, and enter the transfer amount — all without leaving the Dashboard screen.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/dashboard-landing.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 2 — Enter the Amount and Submit**

Enter the dollar amount in the Amount field and click **Transfer** or **Pay** to initiate the transaction immediately. The system validates the available balance in the source account and, if sufficient funds are present, processes the transfer in real time and updates both account balances on the Dashboard.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/quick-transfer-amount.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3 — Confirm the Transfer**

A confirmation message appears on the Dashboard confirming the transfer amount, source and destination accounts, and the effective date. The Upcoming Payments widget and account balance displays refresh immediately to reflect the completed transaction.
