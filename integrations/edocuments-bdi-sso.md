# eDocuments — BDI SSO

**Vendor:** BDI (Broadridge Document Intelligence)\
**Status:** Documented\
**Module:** More Menu — eStatements & Documents

## Overview

The BDI SSO integration enables members to access their electronic statements and tax documents through a Single Sign-On flow from the nFinia app. Members are seamlessly authenticated into the BDI document portal without re-entering credentials.

## Key Features

- Single Sign-On (SSO) from nFinia to BDI document portal
- Access to eStatements, tax documents, and notices
- Document download and print capabilities
- Configurable statement delivery preferences (paper vs. electronic)

## Navigation

**nFinia App:** More Menu → eStatements → View Documents
