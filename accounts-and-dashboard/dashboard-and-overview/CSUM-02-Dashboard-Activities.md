# Dashboard Activities (CSUM-02)

## Summary

The Dashboard Activities view is the primary account monitoring screen in nFinia — combining an account balance summary, a recent transaction feed, and quick-action shortcuts into a single post-login destination. For business members who log in multiple times per day to track cash position, verify payment postings, and monitor for unexpected activity, this screen provides everything needed for a daily account health check without navigating into individual account detail screens.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Dashboard Activities as their daily opening screen — confirming that overnight ACH credits have posted, that scheduled payments have processed, and that account balances are within expected ranges before the business day begins. Members monitoring for fraud use the transaction feed to spot unfamiliar charges immediately on login, triggering an account inquiry before additional unauthorised transactions can occur. Operations staff reviewing a business member's account use the Activities view to see a consolidated snapshot of recent account movement across all linked accounts in a single screen, supporting faster issue identification and resolution.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Start from Dashboard**

You begin at the Dashboard after logging in. The Dashboard displays all account balances, upcoming payments, quick-action tiles, and the top navigation bar with links to Accounts, Move Money, and More.

**Step 2 — Navigate to Move Money Hub**

Click 'Move Money' in the top navigation bar. The Move Money Hub displays all payment and transfer options as tiles including Pay Bills, Quick Pay, Zelle Payment, Internal Transfers, Other You, Same-Day Transfers, Send Instantly, Manage Recipients, Add Recipient, Transaction History, Scheduled Transfers, and P2P Transfer.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/img_dd37cd06feee.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3 — View the Dashboard**

The main Dashboard is displayed after login, showing multiple account cards with current balances, an upcoming payments sidebar on the right, and quick transfer options available in the right panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/img_eb0a7d21423c.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 4 — Use Quick Transfer from Dashboard**

The Accounts page is shown with a detailed list of multiple checking and savings accounts. Each account displays the account name, masked number, and current balance with available action buttons.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/img_d627ced1170e.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 5 — Fill Quick Transfer Details**

The accounts list view is displayed in a scrollable format showing multiple account options with their account types and current balances for selection.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/img_cf945ff36b05.png" alt="" width="340"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 6 — Enter Transfer Amount**

A full Accounts page is shown displaying all account types — checking, savings, credit cards, and loans — organized by category with their respective balances and transaction history links.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/img_50570104e8a7.png" alt="" width="480"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 7 — Confirm Quick Transfer**

The account overview page displays all account types organized by category. Recent transactions and related action buttons are visible for each account entry.
