# Dashboard Activities

## Summary

The Dashboard Activities panel provides members with a real-time snapshot of recent account transactions directly on the landing screen — eliminating the need to navigate into individual accounts just to confirm whether a payment posted or a deposit cleared. For business members tracking daily cash flow, this panel surfaces the most recent debits and credits across all linked accounts the moment they log in, supporting faster decision-making on transfers and funding.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Dashboard Activities to confirm that payroll disbursements have posted, verify incoming wire credits, and spot unexpected charges before they escalate. Members reconciling end-of-day activity use the panel as a quick audit checkpoint without opening individual account detail screens. The activity feed also serves as an early fraud detection signal — an unfamiliar transaction visible immediately on login prompts faster response than a monthly statement review.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Log In and View the Dashboard**

After authentication, the Dashboard loads with the Activities panel displaying your most recent transactions across all linked accounts. Each entry shows the account name, transaction description, date, and amount — debits and credits clearly distinguished — giving you an immediate read on account movement without additional navigation.

**Step 2 — Scroll to Review Recent Activity**

Scroll through the Activities panel to review the full list of recent transactions in reverse chronological order. Click any transaction row to open the Account Detail view for that account, where the full transaction history and balance breakdown are available.
