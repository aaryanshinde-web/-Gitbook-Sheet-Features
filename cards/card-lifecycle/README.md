# Card Lifecycle

Card Lifecycle covers card-level actions that span issuance through retirement — activating a new card, viewing card details, adding a card to a digital wallet (Apple Pay / Google Pay / Samsung Pay), reporting lost or stolen, and replacing or reordering. These flows are available to Summerville CU members across both web and mobile.

## What's in this section

* **Visa Push Provisioning — Link Card to Digital Wallet** — The web-initiated push-provisioning flow that adds a Summerville CU card to the member’s mobile wallet.
* **Add to Digital Wallet (Mobile)** — The in-app Add to Wallet flow on iOS and Android, including the Visa / Mastercard handshake steps.
