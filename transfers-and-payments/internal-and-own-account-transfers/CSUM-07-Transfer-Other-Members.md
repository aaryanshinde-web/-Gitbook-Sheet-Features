# Transfer to Other Members

## Summary

Transfer to Other Members enables members to send funds directly to another Summerville CU member's account — settling instantly within the credit union's core system without requiring the recipient's external account details. For business members paying contractors, sole proprietors, or suppliers who also bank at Summerville CU, this is the fastest and lowest-friction internal transfer path — no routing numbers, no ACH delays, and no wire fees.

## Key Use Cases

Business members use Member-to-Member transfers to pay a contractor or freelancer who banks at Summerville CU, funding their account instantly on the day the work is completed without the standard ACH processing delay. Members use this feature to split a shared expense with a family member or business partner at the same institution — looking up the recipient by name or member number rather than requiring account and routing details. The transfer also supports intra-family account funding scenarios common among Summerville CU members, where a parent regularly funds a college student's checking account within the same institution.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Open Transfer to Other Members**

From Move Money, select **Transfer to Member** to open the member transfer form. Enter the recipient's member number or search by name to locate the destination account — the platform confirms the recipient exists at Summerville CU before allowing the transfer to proceed.

**Step 2 — Select Source Account, Enter Amount, and Add a Memo**

Choose the source account, enter the transfer amount, and optionally add a memo that will appear on both the sender's and recipient's transaction records. The memo field is useful for business members documenting the payment purpose — for example, referencing an invoice number or project code.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (2) (1) (1) (1) (1) (1) (1).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3 — Confirm and Send**

Review the recipient name, source account, amount, and memo on the confirmation screen. Click **Send** to execute the transfer — funds are credited to the recipient's account immediately and both parties receive a transaction notification. The transfer appears in the transaction history for both accounts.
