# Activate New Card

## Summary

Activate New Card allows members to activate a newly issued or replacement debit or credit card directly within nFinia Digital Banking — without calling the activation phone number on the card sticker. For business members who receive a new card as part of onboarding or after a card replacement, digital activation eliminates a manual step and immediately makes the card available for purchases, ATM use, and digital wallet provisioning.

## Key Use Cases

Business members activate a newly issued business debit card through digital banking immediately upon receipt, enabling use the same day without waiting for a phone activation call. Members who receive a replacement card after a fraud event activate the new card and immediately re-add it to Apple Pay or Google Pay — restoring full payment functionality in one session. Operations staff managing card issuance for a business account activate cards on behalf of authorised users directly from the Cards module, confirming activation status before distributing physical cards to employees.

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Locate the Card Awaiting Activation**

From the Cards dashboard, locate the card that shows an **Activate** prompt or a pending-activation status indicator. Cards awaiting activation are distinguished from active cards in the dashboard list — click the card tile or the **Activate Card** link to begin the activation flow.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Cards_List.png" alt="Cards dashboard listing all user cards" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 1: Open the Cards dashboard. For deactivated cards, the row action changes from <strong>Deactivate / Replace Card</strong> to <strong>Activate Card</strong>.</p></figcaption></figure>

**Step 2 — Enter the Card Verification Details**

The activation screen prompts for the last four digits of the card number and, depending on the card type, the CVV or expiration date as verification that the member is in physical possession of the card. Enter the required details and click **Activate**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Activate_Card_Form.png" alt="Activate New Card — enter card details" data-size="original"><figcaption><p>Step 2: Enter your new card details — card number, expiration, and CVV — then click Activate Card.</p></figcaption></figure>

**Step 3 — Confirm Activation**

A confirmation message confirms the card is now active and ready for use. The card status on the Cards dashboard updates immediately — the member can proceed to set a PIN, configure Card Controls, or add the card to a digital wallet from the same screen.
