# External Transfers

External Transfers covers every rail nFinia offers for moving money out of Summerville Credit Union to another financial institution — standard ACH, FedNow instant payments, and domestic wires. Each rail has distinct timing, cost, cutoff, and risk characteristics, which the individual pages document in detail.

## What's in this section

* **External ACH Transfer** — — ACH push and pull transfers to an external linked account, including same-day vs. standard timing.
* **FedNow Instant Pay** — — FedNow-rail instant payments, availability windows, and the receive-side member experience.
* **Wire Transfers** — — domestic wire origination, verification, cutoffs, and fees.
