# Recent Activities & My Insights

> **Module:** Banking › More › Recent Activities / My Insights

## Product Summary

Recent Activities provides a real-time log of all significant account events since your last login — transfers initiated, payments made, login events, alert triggers, and form submissions. It serves as an immediate session security audit, allowing you to quickly verify that no unexpected actions occurred since your previous session.

My Insights delivers personalized spending analytics and financial intelligence based on your transaction history. Transactions are automatically categorized by merchant type, enabling you to see spending breakdowns by category (groceries, dining, utilities, entertainment), income vs. spending trends, and cash flow patterns. The Insights tab provides category-level drill-down with individual transaction detail.

Together, Recent Activities and My Insights give you both a security-focused retrospective (what happened) and a financial intelligence view (how money is being spent) — two complementary lenses on your account activity.

**At a Glance**

| Attribute | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Module | Banking › More › Recent Activities & My Insights |
| Recent Activities | All events since last login in reverse chronological order |
| Insights Categorization | Automatic merchant category code-based transaction grouping |
| Insights Views | Overview (income vs. spend), Insights tab (category breakdown) |
| Data Source | Transaction history across all accounts in active membership |

## Key Use Cases

| Use Case | Who Uses It | What They Do | Business Value |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Post-Login Security Check | You verifying no unauthorized activity | Review Recent Activities immediately after login | Instant detection of unauthorized transfers or login events |
| Monthly Spending Review | You reviewing your spending categories | Open My Insights for category-level spending breakdown | Identifies over-spending areas at a glance without manual ledger review |
| Income vs. Spending Comparison | You monitoring monthly cash flow | View Overview in My Insights for income/expense totals | Quick cash flow health check with no manual calculation required |
| Transaction Audit | You researching where money was spent | Drill into a spending category in Insights to see all transactions | Data-driven basis for identifying and reducing discretionary spending |
