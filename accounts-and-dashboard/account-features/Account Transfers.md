# Account Transfers

## Summary

Members can initiate a transfer directly from any account in the Account Overview by clicking the "Transfers" button on the account row. This action navigates to the Transfer Funds to Own Account page, pre-populated with the selected account as the "From" account. The member selects a destination account from the available options, enters the transfer amount, specifies transfer frequency (one-time or recurring), and confirms the transfer. This streamlined workflow allows quick transfers without navigating separately to the Move Money section.

## Key Use Cases

- Transfer funds between own accounts from Account Overview
- Move funds to a savings account after reviewing balances
- Quick transfer without navigating to dedicated transfer page
- Set up recurring transfers between accounts
- Consolidate funds across account types

## End-to-End Workflow

**Step 1: Initiate transfer from Account Overview**

The member clicks "Accounts" in the top navigation bar to open the Account Overview page. All accounts are listed with their balances and action buttons. The member clicks "Transfers" on any account row. The Transfer Funds to Own Account page loads with the selected account pre-set as the "From" account. The member then selects the destination account from the "To" dropdown menu, showing all other eligible accounts available for transfer.

**Step 2: Enter transfer details and confirm**

The member enters the transfer amount in the Amount field. The member selects the transfer frequency from the available options (one-time, weekly, monthly, etc.). The member reviews the transfer details and clicks "Continue" to proceed to confirmation. The system confirms the transfer and displays a success message, with the transaction now scheduled to process.
